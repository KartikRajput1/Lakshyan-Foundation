Program: ‘Namma’ Chess Scholarship Program

Athlete Name: Mr. Apoorv Kamble

Sport: Chess

Scholarship period: Since May 2023

Program Overview:

  • Fully funded scholarship program
  • Provide coaching sessions under the guidance of expert coaches at no cost
  • Cover travel and stay expenses for shortlisted international tournaments
  • Mentorship and guidance from chess grandmasters
  • Access to state-of-the-art facilities for the best coaching

Athlete Achievements:

  • Became state Senior blitz, U15 Open & U19 Open champion during 2023-24
  • Gained Candidate Master (CM) title in September 2023
  • Gained Fide Master (FM) title in February 2024
  • India Active players ranking - moved up from >500 to 119th place in India
  • Karnataka State Blitz Rated Chess Championship 2023 - 1st Place
  • MDCC 1st International FIDE Rated Open Chess Tournament 2023 - 1st Place
  • 1st Namma Sub Junior Karnataka U15 Rapid Chess Tournament 2023 - 1st Place
  • Karnataka State U-19 & U15 Fide Rated Open Chess Championship 2023 - 1st Place
  • 52nd National Junior (U-19) Chess Championship 2023 - 6th Place
  • 1st Bangalore International Grandmasters Open Chess Tournament 2024 (Category A) - 2nd Place (Drew with 2 Grandmasters & 2 International Masters)
  • National Rapid Chess Championship 2023-24 - 39th Place
  • Overall increase: 388 Standard points, 204 Rapid Blitz points & 328 Blitz rating points
